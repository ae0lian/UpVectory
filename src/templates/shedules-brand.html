Brand Guidelines for
ShedulesPro

The idea of these guidelines was to create a clear neutral style, that makes the SchedulesPro app attractive for all their users.

The concept of the logo is to combine all professionals and their clients who use the online booking of the services.

Colors, typography and entire style had to be clear, light, attractive and modern as it is still a Start-up.
