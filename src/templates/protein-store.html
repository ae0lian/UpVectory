PROTEIN.COM.UA

It was a very ambitious project. We have managed the full-cycle of works, from the naming to web wow slideInRightdevelopment and testing.

We expected high traffic and hundreds of goods in the store. So both the front-end and back-end should have to work excellent.

After we find the ambitious enough name, we had a challenging case to buy the domain name from the previous owner.

Then, we have to design a logo and concept.

The logo icon is a protein molecule. The entire style is green and light and says that here in this store you can buy healthy sports nutrition.
